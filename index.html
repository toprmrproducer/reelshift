Private video conversion

Convert video files, without uploading anything.

ReelShift converts between MP4, WebM, GIF, and extracts MP3 audio — entirely inside your browser. A real build of FFmpeg runs on WebAssembly, right on your device. Nothing you select is ever sent anywhere.

You can select more than one file — they'll be converted one after another.

The whole batch converts to this one format.

Idle0%

Supported conversions:any browser-playable video source can be converted to MP4 (H.264 video, AAC-compatible container), WebM, an animated GIF (downscaled to 480px wide at 10fps for a reasonable file size), or MP3 audio extracted from the video's soundtrack. The first time you convert something, your browser downloads a small (~30MB) conversion engine and caches it — every conversion after that starts instantly. Very large files or exotic codecs may take longer or fail depending on your device's memory. Files in a batch are converted one at a time, in order, so memory use stays bounded regardless of queue size.
