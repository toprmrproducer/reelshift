Privacy Policy

Files you select in ReelShift are processed entirely on your own device, inside your browser. They are never uploaded to, or stored on, any server operated by ReelShift.

ReelShift uses standard web analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, referrers) and may display advertising through Google AdSense, which can set its own cookies subject to Google's own privacy policy.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the details on the About page.

All conversions run locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
